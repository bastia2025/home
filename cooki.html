Cookie Policy

ENG ("we," "us," or "our") operates a platform offering English language courses (the "Platform"). This Cookie Policy explains how we use cookies and similar tracking technologies when you visit our website, mobile applications, or related services. By using the Platform, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. For information on how we handle your personal data, please see our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website or use an online service. They help websites recognize your device, store preferences, and enhance your user experience. We also use similar technologies, such as web beacons, pixels, and local storage, which serve comparable purposes.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

We use the following types of cookies on the Platform:

a. Essential Cookies

  • Purpose: These cookies are necessary for the Platform to function properly, enabling core features like navigation, account access, and course enrollment.
  • Examples: Authentication cookies, session cookies.
  • Retention: Typically deleted when you close your browser (session cookies) or after a short period.

b. Functional Cookies

  • Purpose: These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your preferences, such as language settings or course progress.
  • Examples: Cookies that save your language choice (e.g., English or Arabic) or track your course completion status.
  • Retention: May persist for weeks or months, depending on the feature.

c. Analytics Cookies

  • Purpose: These cookies help us understand how users interact with the Platform, such as which pages are visited or how long users spend on courses, so we can improve our services.
  • Examples: Cookies used by tools like Google Analytics to track page views and user behavior.
  • Retention: Varies (e.g., up to 2 years for Google Analytics).

d. Marketing Cookies

  • Purpose: These cookies enable personalized advertisements or promotional content, such as course recommendations, based on your interests and browsing behavior.
  • Examples: Cookies set by third-party advertising networks to display relevant ads.
  • Retention: Varies depending on the third-party provider.

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the Platform operates smoothly and securely (e.g., user authentication, secure payments).
  • Personalize your experience, such as remembering your language preference or course progress.
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve Platform functionality and content.
  • Deliver relevant promotions or advertisements, including through third-party partners.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use, such as:

  • Payment Processors: To facilitate secure transactions.
  • Analytics Providers: To collect usage data (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Advertising Partners: To display personalized ads on or off the Platform. These third parties may place cookies on your device and collect data subject to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies for details.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control how cookies are used:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Visit your browser’s help section for instructions. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect Platform functionality.
  • Cookie Consent Tool: On your first visit, you may see a cookie consent banner allowing you to accept or customize your cookie preferences (except for essential cookies, which cannot be disabled).
  • Opting Out of Ads: For marketing cookies, you can opt out via third-party tools like the Network Advertising Initiative (http://optout.networkadvertising.org) or Your Online Choices (http://www.youronlinechoices.com).

6. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) feature. As there is no universal standard for DNT, our Platform does not currently respond to DNT signals. However, you can manage cookies through your browser settings or our cookie consent tool.

7. Data Protection and Cookies

Cookies that collect personal information are subject to our Privacy Policy. We use industry-standard security measures to protect cookie data, but no online transmission is 100% secure.

8. Updates to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated by posting the updated policy on the Platform or via email.
