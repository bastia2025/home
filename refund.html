Refund Policy

ENG ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to providing high-quality English language courses through our platform (the "Platform"). This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which you may request a refund for course purchases or subscriptions. By enrolling in a course or subscribing to our services, you agree to the terms of this Refund Policy.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

1.1 Course Purchases

Refund Window: You may request a refund for a paid course within 14 days of purchase, provided you have not completed more than 20% of the course content (e.g., lessons, quizzes, or assignments).

Non-Refundable Courses: Certain courses may be marked as non-refundable at the time of purchase. This will be clearly indicated during the checkout process.

Free Trials: If a course offers a free trial, no payment is charged until the trial period ends. You may cancel during the trial without incurring charges.

1.2 Subscriptions

Refund Window: For subscription-based access to the Platform (e.g., monthly or annual plans), you may request a refund within 14 days of your initial subscription payment, provided you have not accessed more than 20% of any subscribed course content.
Recurring Payments: No refunds will be provided for subscription payments after the initial 14-day period or for partial billing periods (e.g., mid-month or mid-year cancellations).

Cancellation: You may cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing period, and no further charges will be applied.

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Courses or subscriptions marked as non-refundable at the time of purchase.
  • Courses where more than 20% of the content has been accessed or completed.
  • Gift cards, promotional credits, or discounted purchases, unless otherwise specified.
  • Fees associated with third-party services (e.g., payment processing fees).

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@engplatform.com within the eligible refund period.
  2. Provide your account details, purchase information (e.g., order number), and the reason for your refund request.
  3. Our team will review your request and confirm eligibility based on this Refund Policy.
  4. Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days to the original payment method.

4. Exceptions

Technical Issues

If you experience technical issues that prevent access to a course or the Platform, please contact support@engplatform.com. We will attempt to resolve the issue. If the issue cannot be resolved, we may offer a refund or account credit at our discretion.

Special Circumstances

In exceptional cases (e.g., documented medical emergencies), we may consider refund requests outside the standard eligibility criteria. Such requests will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

5. Refunds for Canceled or Modified Courses

  • If ENG cancels a course before it begins, you will receive a full refund or an option to transfer to a comparable course.
  • If significant changes are made to a course (e.g., content or access restrictions) after your enrollment, and these changes materially affect your experience, you may request a refund within 14 days of the change.

6. Payment Disputes

If you believe a charge was made in error, contact us at support@engplatform.com before initiating a chargeback with your payment provider. Chargebacks without prior communication may result in account suspension.

7. Updates to This Refund Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated by posting the updated policy on the Platform or via email. Your continued use of the Platform after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.
